Alternative Mode of Scholarship Award - American Association of Geographers, Urban Geography Specialty Group

West Side Property Condition Inventory

Research project investigating changing housing conditions in a gentrifying urban neighborhood.

Project consisted of:

  • Geodatabase design
  • ArcGIS Collector survey design and data collection
  • Spatial analysis of field-collected data
  • Creation of a public data website, consisting of a Story Map and interactive web map developed using ArcGIS JavaScript API
  • Publication of a paper presenting the project as an example of "Community Geography" aimed at urban planners and community development practitioners
