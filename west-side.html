West Side Property Condition Inventory

Research project investigating changing housing conditions in a gentrifying urban neighborhood.

Project consisted of survey design and data collection, spatial data analysis of field-collected data, and design of a public data website and web map.

Alternative Mode of Scholarship Award - American Association of Geographers, Urban Geography Specialty Group

ArcGIS Collector, HTML/CSS, ArcGIS JavaScript API, Google Maps API, Story Maps
