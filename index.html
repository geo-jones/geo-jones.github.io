I'm Paula, a GIS specialist
interested in maps, spatial data,
and web development.

My work encompasses GIS, Data Wrangling, Web Map Development, and Visualization.

I consider myself a problem-solver first and foremost, with GIS and geospatial techniques as tools in my toolbox. My software competencies range from Esri's suite of products (Desktop, Online, Enterprise, field apps) to open-source desktop (QGIS) and relational database systems (PostgreSQL, PostGIS). I also have experience utilizing Python, JavaScript, HTML, SQL, and R languages.

In my free time, you can find me on the pitch refereeing and playing soccer, or exploring city and trail by bike.

I am always open to new contract work opportunities and collaborations, so please feel free to reach out.
